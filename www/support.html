Support

We're here to help

Need help? Email us or use the in-app feedback button. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Get in Touch

For general questions, suggestions, or anything else, email us at hello@tablefortwo.uk.

We aim to respond to all enquiries within 2 business days.

Report an Issue

Found a bug or something not working as expected? You can:

  • Use the in-app feedback button — tap "Something wrong? Report an issue" at the bottom of the Discover page, or look for "Report this issue" links throughout the app.
  • Email us directly at hello@tablefortwo.uk with a description of what happened.

Please include as much detail as possible so we can investigate quickly.

Account & Data

For account deletion requests, data access requests, or any privacy-related enquiries, email privacy@tablefortwo.uk.

You can also delete your account directly from the app — go to the Account section on the Profiles page and tap "Delete my account".

For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.

FAQs

How do I delete my account?
Open the app, go to the Profiles page, scroll to the Account section at the bottom, and tap "Delete my account". Your data will be removed within 30 days. You can also email privacy@tablefortwo.uk.
Is Table for Two free?
Yes, Table for Two is free to use. We help you discover and plan dates — any costs come from the venues and services you choose to book.
Which cities are supported?
We currently support London, with more cities coming soon.
