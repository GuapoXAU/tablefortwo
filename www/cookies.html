Cookie Notice

Last updated: 26 May 2026

What we use

Table for Two is a web app. We primarily use browser local storage rather than traditional cookies. Here's what's stored and why.

Essential storage (always active)

These are required for the app to work. They cannot be disabled.

KeyPurposeDuration
Supabase auth tokensKeep you signed inSession / 1 week
t4t_user_profileYour name, preferences, and settings (offline cache)Persistent
t4t_bk, t4t_rmYour bookings and reminders (offline cache)Persistent
t4t_venue_cacheCached venue data to reduce loading times15 minutes
t4t_cookie_consentYour cookie/analytics preferencePersistent

Optional analytics (consent required)

These are only loaded if you click "Accept" on the consent banner.

ServicePurposeWhat it collects
Vercel Web AnalyticsUnderstand which pages are visited and how the app performsPage views, device type, country (no personal identifiers)
SentryDetect and fix crashes and errorsError messages, browser info, anonymous session ID
Product usage eventsUnderstand which features are usedFeature interactions, anonymous user ID

How to manage your preferences

  • On first visit: A consent banner lets you Accept or Reject optional analytics.
  • Change your mind: Open the app menu and tap "Privacy settings" to update your choice at any time.
  • Clear everything: Clear your browser's site data to remove all local storage.

Third-party cookies

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels. When you click through to external booking sites, those sites may set their own cookies — please refer to their own cookie policies.
